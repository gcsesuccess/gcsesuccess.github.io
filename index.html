Free GCSE English and Maths tutoring in Battersea

GCSE Success is a volunteer-led tutoring and mentoring project that offers free one-on-one Maths and English tuition to GCSE students in the Latchmere ward of Wandsworth. It is intended to advance the education and opportunities of young people in year 10 or 11 of local state schools and living on the surrounding York Road, Kampala and Winstanley estates. The scheme started in 2012 and is overseen by a Management Committee.

GCSE Success takes place weekly:

  •   Saturdays 10am-12pm
  •   York Gardens Library, Battersea
